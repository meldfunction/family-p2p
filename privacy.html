Privacy Policy

We protect your data with industry-leading privacy technology

Last Updated: January 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Kith is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. We collect only what's necessary to provide the service, we never sell your data, and we use privacy-preserving technology to protect you even from ourselves.

Privacy-Preserving Technology

Kith implements zero-knowledge encryption and differential privacy to ensure that even we cannot access or misuse your sensitive data. Here's how:

  • Zero-Knowledge Proofs: We can verify certain actions (like completed exchanges) without seeing the underlying data. Your privacy is mathematically guaranteed.
  • End-to-End Encryption: Messages between members are encrypted on your device. We cannot read them.
  • Differential Privacy: When we analyze usage patterns to improve the app, we add cryptographic noise that makes individual data non-attributable while preserving aggregate insights.
  • Local-First Architecture: Most data lives on your device. Our servers store only what's needed for coordination.

Bottom line: Even if compelled by law, we cannot hand over data we cryptographically cannot access.

What We Collect & Why

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email, location (city/region), phone number (optional for notifications)
  • Profile Information: Skills you offer, needs you have, availability preferences
  • Network Connections: Who you've invited to your circle, who invited you
  • Exchange Logs: Time credits earned/spent, services exchanged, dates of exchanges
  • Messages: End-to-end encrypted communications between members (we cannot read these)
  • Payment Information: If using paid services, payment details processed by Stripe (we don't store credit card numbers)

Why we need this: To match you with complementary skills, track reciprocity, facilitate coordination, and process payments when requested.

Information We Generate

  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage, session duration, error logs (all aggregated and anonymized via differential privacy)
  • Trust Scores: Reciprocity balance, reliability metrics, exchange completion rates (visible to your circle only)
  • Matching Algorithms: Suggestions for who might help with specific needs based on skills/availability

Why we generate this: To improve the app, prevent abuse, and help you find relevant connections.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Browsing history outside the app
  • Location tracking beyond city/region
  • Third-party advertising data
  • Biometric data
  • Social media profiles or contact lists (unless you explicitly share)

How We Use Your Data

We Use Your Data To:

  • Provide and improve Kith's core services (time banking, skill matching, coordination)
  • Process payments for optional paid services
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure trust & safety
  • Send you service notifications (exchange confirmations, credit updates, safety alerts)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations (only when required by law)

We Will NEVER:

  • Sell your data to advertisers, data brokers, or third parties
  • Use your data to train AI models for other companies
  • Share your network connections with anyone outside your circle
  • Track you across the web with cookies or pixels
  • Show you targeted ads based on your activity
  • Share exchange details with anyone except the parties involved

Data Sharing & Disclosure

Within Your Circle

Members of your circle can see:

  • Your name, profile photo, and bio
  • Skills you've listed as available
  • Your reciprocity balance with them (not with others)
  • Exchange history between you and them
  • Mutual connections (who vouched for whom)

With Service Providers

We share minimal data with:

  • Stripe: Payment processing for optional paid services
  • AWS/Cloud Infrastructure: Encrypted data storage and hosting
  • Twilio: SMS notifications (if you opt in)

All service providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and cannot use your data for their own purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required by law (subpoena, court order, etc.), but only:

  • The minimum data necessary to comply
  • After notifying you (unless prohibited by law)
  • After challenging overly broad requests

Important: Due to our zero-knowledge architecture, much of your data is cryptographically inaccessible even to us. We cannot hand over what we cannot access.

Your Rights & Controls

You Can Always:

  • Export your data: Download all your exchange history, messages, network connections in standard formats
  • Delete your account: Full deletion within 30 days (some data retained for legal/tax purposes if you used paid services)
  • Control visibility: Choose what's visible to your circle vs. extended network
  • Opt out of communications: Disable notifications, unsubscribe from newsletters
  • Request corrections: Fix inaccurate data in your profile
  • Limit data processing: Request we stop processing certain data types (may limit functionality)

Contact privacy@kith.app to exercise any of these rights.

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days, some retained for legal compliance (7 years for tax/payment records)
  • Backups: Deleted data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days
  • Aggregated analytics: Anonymized usage data retained indefinitely for product improvement

Security Measures

We protect your data with:

  • End-to-end encryption for messages
  • Zero-knowledge proofs for sensitive exchanges
  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Two-factor authentication (optional but recommended)
  • Anomaly detection for suspicious activity

No system is 100% secure. If we detect a breach, we'll notify affected users within 72 hours and provide guidance on protective measures.

Children's Privacy

Kith is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided us with data, contact privacy@kith.app and we'll delete it immediately.

International Users

Kith is based in the United States. If you're outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. We comply with GDPR for EU users and provide equivalent protections for all users globally.

Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you of material changes via email and in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy. You can always export your data and delete your account if you disagree.
