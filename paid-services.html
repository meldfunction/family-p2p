Pricing & fees

Network coordination

Always free. Time banking, messaging, network management‚Äîno charge, ever.

Direct member payments

Members set their own rates. Village Network takes 5% processing fee (covers payment processing + platform costs).

Provider network bookings

Providers set their rates (competitive with local market). Village Network takes 15% to cover vetting, insurance, and platform costs.

Emergency backup care

Premium rates for same-day emergency care from vetted providers. Typically 20-30% higher than standard rates.
