How Village Network solves the 7am panic

When childcare falls through at the last minute, here's exactly what happens

The Crisis Scenario

Tuesday, 7:05am

The crisis

Sarah's babysitter texts: "So sorry, I'm sick and can't come today." Sarah has a critical work presentation at 9am. Her 6-year-old daughter Emma needs care for the next 4 hours.

7:06am

The request

Sarah opens Village Network and posts: "Emergency! Need childcare 8am-12pm today. Emma is 6, loves art and books. Can return favor this weekend or pay forward to network."

7:09am

The response

Lisa (3 blocks away) sees it immediately: "I can take her! My daughter's the same age. They can do crafts together. Drop her off anytime." Marcus (further out) offers backup if needed.

Result

The outcome

Lisa: Gains +4hr credit, girls play together
Emma: Plays with friend instead of sitting in office
System: Tracks exchange automatically
Next time: Lisa can call in the favor, guilt-free
