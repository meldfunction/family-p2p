When you need a break

Don't grab a Snickers. Grab your Kith.

Modern parents are trying to do what 8-14 people used to share

For 200,000 years, children were raised by extended family, neighbors, and community. Then we scattered everyone, isolated families in separate houses, and expected 1-2 exhausted people to handle everything.

The burnout you feel isn't personal failure. It's a rational response to an impossible setup.
